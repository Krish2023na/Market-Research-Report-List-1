Short Arm pumps are useful for small to medium construction projects, while Long Arm pumps are suitable for taller structures. <p><ul><li>Line Pumps</li><li>Boom Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck-mounted concrete pumps market includes two main types of pumps: line pumps and boom pumps. Line pumps are generally used for small to medium-sized construction projects, where a lower volume of concrete needs to be delivered. They are highly versatile, suitable for both horizontal and vertical pumping. On the other hand, boom pumps are ideal for large construction projects with higher concrete volumes. They consist of a robotic arm (boom) that can reach elevated or remote areas, making them suitable for tall buildings or hard-to-reach areas.</p></p>
