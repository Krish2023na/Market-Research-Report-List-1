<p><p>The Green Steel market can be categorized into two types: one produced by Electric Arc Furnace (EAF) and the other produced by Molten Oxide Electrolysis (MOE). The EAF method involves the use of electricity to melt scrap steel, reducing the carbon emissions and energy consumption compared to traditional methods. On the other hand, MOE utilizes an electrolytic process to produce steel, using renewable energy and avoiding carbon emissions altogether. Both methods contribute to the production of environmentally friendly steel but employ different techniques to achieve it.</p></p>
