<p><p>Cell therapy isolators are specialized equipment used in the field of regenerative medicine to ensure a controlled and sterile environment for cell processing and manufacturing. Multi-chamber isolators are isolators with multiple compartments to separate different processes, preventing contamination. These isolators allow simultaneous operations with different cell lines. Double chamber isolators, on the other hand, have two interconnected compartments, providing a seamless workflow from cell loading to the final product. Both types of isolators play a crucial role in maintaining sterile conditions and maximizing productivity in cell therapy manufacturing.</p></p>
