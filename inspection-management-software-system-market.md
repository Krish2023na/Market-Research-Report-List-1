<p><p>Inspection management software systems offer two main types of market solutions: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based systems are accessed via the internet and hosted on remote servers, allowing users to store, manage, and access data from any location. This offers flexibility, scalability, and eliminates the need for local infrastructure or maintenance. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed and run on local servers or computers within an organization's premises. It provides complete control over data and security but requires hardware setup, maintenance, and has limited accessibility.</p></p>
