<p><p>The Aluminium Nitride Substrate Market encompasses different types including Direct Bonded Copper (DBC), Direct Plated Copper (DPC), Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic (LTCC), and High-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic (HTCC). DBC refers to the process of directly bonding copper to the aluminium nitride substrate, enabling efficient heat dissipation. DPC involves directly plating copper on the substrate, providing electrical conductivity. LTCC is a method where multiple layers of ceramic and metal are stacked together at low temperatures. HTCC involves co-firing ceramic layers with metal conductors at high temperatures for enhanced performance and integration. These diverse types cater to various applications in electronics and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
