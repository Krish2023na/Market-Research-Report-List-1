<p><strong>The Polymer Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Modified Concrete (PMC)</li><li>Polymer Resin Concrete (PC)</li><li>Polymer Impregnated Concrete (PIC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer Concrete market consists of three main types: Polymer Modified Concrete (PMC), Polymer Resin Concrete (PC), and Polymer Impregnated Concrete (PIC). PMC is created by adding polymers to the concrete mix to enhance its strength and durability. PC is formed by replacing the cement binder with polymer resin, resulting in improved chemical resistance. PIC involves coating the concrete with a polymer resin to improve its surface properties. All three types of polymer concrete offer advantages such as increased longevity, resistance to chemicals, and reduced maintenance needs.</p></p>
