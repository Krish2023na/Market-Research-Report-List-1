The IoHT market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based IoHT involves storing and accessing healthcare data and applications on remote servers through the internet, providing flexibility and scalability. On-premises IoHT, on the other hand, involves deploying and managing healthcare infrastructure locally within an organization's premises, offering greater control and security but with potentially higher upfront costs.</p></p>
