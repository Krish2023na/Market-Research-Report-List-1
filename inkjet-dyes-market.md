<p><p>The inkjet dyes market comprises two main types: liquids and powders. Liquid inkjet dyes are available in the form of liquid solutions, which are commonly used in inkjet printers. These dyes usually provide vibrant and high-quality prints, as they easily dissolve in the ink and distribute evenly on the printing surface. On the other hand, powder inkjet dyes are finely ground particles that need to be mixed with a liquid solvent before being used. They offer flexibility in terms of color intensity and are often used in specialized printing applications.</p></p>
