<p><p>The Automotive Platooning System market involves two key types: Vehicle to Vehicle (V2V) and Vehicle to Infrastructure (V2I). In V2V, vehicles form a communication network and exchange data with each other, enabling them to travel in close proximity while maintaining safe distances. This helps in coordinated driving, improving traffic flow, and reducing accidents. V2I, on the other hand, involves communication between vehicles and surrounding infrastructure such as traffic lights or road sensors. This enables vehicles to receive real-time traffic information and adapt their behavior accordingly, enhancing safety and efficiency on the roads.</p></p>
