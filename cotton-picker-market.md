<p><p>Cotton picker market types can be categorized as spindle picker and stripper market. Spindle picker refers to a type of cotton picker that operates by using spindles or bristles to selectively remove the cotton from the plant while leaving the rest of the plant intact. On the other hand, the stripper market involves the use of a machine that strips the entire cotton boll and some accompanying plant material from the plant. Both types provide different methods of cotton harvesting, allowing farmers to choose the one that best suits their needs.</p></p>
