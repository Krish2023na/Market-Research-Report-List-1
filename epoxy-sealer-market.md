<p><p>The epoxy sealer market consists of two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based epoxy sealers are produced by mixing water with epoxy resin, resulting in a product that is non-toxic, low odor, and environmentally friendly. These sealers are primarily used for indoor applications and are known for their ease of use and quick drying time. On the other hand, solvent-based epoxy sealers are produced by mixing epoxy resin with solvents, making them more durable and resistant to chemicals and abrasions. These sealers are mainly used for outdoor applications and are known for their high performance and long-lasting protection.</p></p>
