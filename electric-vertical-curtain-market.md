<p><p>The Electric Vertical Curtain market offers two types of controls: Infrared Remote Control and Radio Remote Control. Infrared remote control utilizes infrared signals to wirelessly communicate with the curtain, allowing users to operate it from a distance using a handheld remote. Both types of controls provide convenient and efficient ways to operate electric vertical curtains based on the user's preferences.</p></p>
