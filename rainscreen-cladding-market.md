<p><p>Rainscreen cladding is a type of cladding system that is designed to provide a protective outer layer to a building's exterior walls, while also allowing for appropriate ventilation and moisture management. This can be attributed to factors such as increasing construction activities in both residential and commercial sectors, growing awareness regarding energy-efficient building materials, and the rising focus on improving the aesthetic appeal of buildings. Additionally, the need for effective moisture management and thermal insulation in buildings has also contributed to the market growth.</p><p>In terms of materials, the rainscreen cladding market is segmented into metal, fiber cement, composite materials, terracotta, and others.
