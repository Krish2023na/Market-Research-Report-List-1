<p><p>The handheld medical scanner market includes two types: wire and wireless scanners. Wire scanners are connected to a device through a physical wire, allowing for data transfer and power supply. They typically provide a stable connection but may restrict mobility. On the other hand, wireless scanners operate without wires, enabling mobility and ease of use.
