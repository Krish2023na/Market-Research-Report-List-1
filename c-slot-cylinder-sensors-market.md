<p><p>C-Slot Cylinder Sensors are a type of proximity sensor used for detecting the position of a piston in a pneumatic cylinder. These sensors work by utilizing an internal reed switch or hall effect sensor to detect the presence of a magnetic field, which is generated by a magnet installed inside the cylinder piston. When the piston moves and the magnet passes by the sensor, it generates an output signal indicating the position of the piston.</p><p>The C-Slot Cylinder Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period.
