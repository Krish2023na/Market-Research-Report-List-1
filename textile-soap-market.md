<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The textile soap market can be categorized into four types: anion, cation, zwitterion, and nonionic. Anion soaps have a negatively charged molecule, making them effective in removing dirt and stains. Cation soaps are positively charged and are suitable for certain materials like silk and wool. Zwitterion soaps have both positive and negative charges, providing a balanced cleaning effect. Nonionic soaps are uncharged and work well with synthetic fibers. These soap types play a significant role in the textile industry by offering various cleaning properties tailored for different fabrics.</p></p>
