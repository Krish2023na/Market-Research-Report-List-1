<p><p>The eco-friendly turf market includes two main types: PE Synthetic Turf and PP Synthetic Turf. PE Synthetic Turf is made from polyethylene, a durable and recyclable material, and is known for its softness and natural appearance. On the other hand, PP Synthetic Turf is made from polypropylene, which is also recyclable but not as durable as PE. Both types of synthetic turf offer a sustainable alternative to natural grass, reducing water consumption, maintenance costs, and CO2 emissions.</p></p>
