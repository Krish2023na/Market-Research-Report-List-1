<p><p>The air brake system market consists of two types: air disc brake and air drum brake. Air disc brakes use a rotor and caliper to apply friction and stop the vehicle. They provide better heat dissipation and offer improved braking performance, making them popular in commercial vehicles. On the other hand, air drum brakes use a rotating drum and brake shoes to stop the vehicle. They are commonly used in heavy-duty trucks and trailers due to their cost-effectiveness and reliability. These two types cater to different vehicle requirements in the air brake system market.</p></p>
