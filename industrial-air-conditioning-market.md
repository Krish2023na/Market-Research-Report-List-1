<p><strong>The Industrial Air Conditioning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Systems</li><li>Local Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial air conditioning market can be classified into two main types: central systems and local systems. Central systems involve a centralized cooling unit that is connected to multiple areas within a building through a network of ducts and vents. This allows for uniform cooling throughout the entire building. On the other hand, local systems consist of individual cooling units installed in specific areas or rooms, providing independent control over the cooling in each space. Both types have their advantages and are suitable for different industrial settings based on the cooling requirements and infrastructure of the building.</p></p>
