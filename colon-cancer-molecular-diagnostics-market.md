<p><p>Colon cancer molecular diagnostics market includes various types such as polymerase chain reaction (PCR), fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH), DNA sequencing, and gene chip market. PCR is a technique used to amplify specific DNA sequences, allowing their detection. FISH is a method used to visualize specific DNA sequences within cells. DNA sequencing determines the order of nucleotides in a DNA molecule. Gene chips are glass slides or silicon chips containing thousands of DNA sequences used to analyze gene expression patterns. These techniques aid in early detection, diagnosis, and monitoring of colon cancer.</p></p>
