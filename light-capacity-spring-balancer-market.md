<p><p>A light capacity spring balancer is a mechanical tool that is used to suspend loads in industrial settings, making it easier for workers to handle and manipulate heavy objects. It works on the principle of counterbalancing the weight of the load with the help of a spring mechanism, providing a constant tension force to offset the load. This allows for effortless movement of the load in vertical or horizontal directions, reducing workers' fatigue and improving productivity.</p><p>The light capacity spring balancer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
