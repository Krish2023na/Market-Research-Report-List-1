<p><p>The automotive door armrest switch market comprises two types: button type and induction type. Button type switches are typically found on older models and require physical pressure to activate. On the other hand, induction type switches are more modern and operate based on proximity. When the driver or passenger's hand approaches the switch, it automatically activates without the need for physical contact. Both types serve the same purpose of controlling the functions of the car's windows, locks, and mirrors, but differ in their operation.</p></p>
