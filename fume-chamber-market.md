<p><strong>The Fume Chamber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Chamber</li><li>Ducted Fume Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The fume chamber market consists of two main types: ductless fume chambers and ducted fume chambers. Ductless fume chambers are designed to filter and recirculate air within the chamber, eliminating the need for external ventilation systems. They are commonly used in laboratories or facilities without access to exhaust ducts. On the other hand, ducted fume chambers are connected to external exhaust systems, effectively removing hazardous fumes and maintaining air quality. This type is often used in industries where strong and continuous ventilation is required to ensure the safety of workers and the environment.</p></p>
