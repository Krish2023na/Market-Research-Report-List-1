All-steel tires are constructed with multiple steel belts and steel plies, providing excellent strength, durability, and resistance to punctures. Semi-steel tires have a combination of steel belts and fabric plies, offering a balance between strength and flexibility. The other market includes tires made with different materials such as nylon, polyester, or aramid fibers. These tires are often used in specific applications or marketed as specialized products, offering unique features like enhanced fuel efficiency or off-road capabilities.</p></p>
