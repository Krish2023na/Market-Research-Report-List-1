<p><p>The titanium carbide market is categorized into two types: powder and granules. Titanium carbide powder refers to fine particles of the compound that are used in various applications like coatings, cutting tools, and electronic devices. It offers excellent hardness and wear resistance properties. On the other hand, titanium carbide granules are larger-sized particles, commonly used in the production of cermets, metal matrix composites, and other specialized applications that require enhanced mechanical properties. Both types play a crucial role in diverse industries, contributing to the overall growth of the titanium carbide market.</p></p>
