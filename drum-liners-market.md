<p><p>The drum liners market consists of three main types: flexible drum liner, rigid drum liner, and semi-rigid drum liner. Flexible drum liners are made of materials like polyethylene and can easily adapt to the shape of the drum. Rigid drum liners are typically made of materials like steel or plastic and provide sturdy support for the contents of the drum. Semi-rigid drum liners offer a balance between flexibility and rigidity, providing moderate support while still allowing for some adaptability. These different types cater to various industries and applications requiring varied levels of protection.</p></p>
