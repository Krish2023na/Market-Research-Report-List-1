<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected</li><li>Combiner Projected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive heads-up display (HUD) market can be categorized into two types: windshield projected and combiner projected. In windshield projected HUDs, the relevant information is displayed directly onto the windshield, allowing the driver to view it without taking their eyes off the road. Combiner projected HUDs, on the other hand, use a small transparent screen that reflects the information onto the driver's line of sight. Both types provide important information such as speed, navigation, and warnings, enhancing the driving experience and safety.</p></p>
