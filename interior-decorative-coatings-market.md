<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Interior decorative coatings market is classified into two main types: waterborne and solvent-borne coatings. Waterborne coatings are water-based and their formulations contain water as a major solvent. They have low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them more environmentally friendly and safer to use. On the other hand, solvent-borne coatings are based on organic solvents and have higher levels of VOCs. Although solvent-borne coatings provide excellent performance and durability, they have a negative impact on air quality and present health and safety risks.</p></p>
