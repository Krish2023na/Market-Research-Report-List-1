<p><p>The automotive active safety market includes various types of systems aimed at improving driver and passenger safety. Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheels from locking up during braking, improving control and stability. Driver Monitoring System (DMS) monitors the driver's attention and fatigue level to prevent accidents. Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) automatically adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe distance from the preceding vehicle. Lane Departure Warning System (LDWS) alerts the driver when unintentionally straying from the lane. Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) monitors tire pressure to ensure optimal safety. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) prevents skidding and loss of control. Blind Spot Detection (BSD) warns the driver about vehicles in blind spots. Night Vision System (NVS) enhances visibility in low-light conditions.</p></p>
