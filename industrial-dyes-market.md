<p><p>The industrial dyes market is categorized into various types, including disperse dyes, reactive dyes, sulfur dyes, vat dyes, acid dyes, and others. Disperse dyes are used mainly for dyeing synthetic fibers such as polyester. Reactive dyes are chemically reactive and bond with natural fibers like cotton. Sulfur dyes have a distinctive color range and are commonly used for dyeing cotton. Vat dyes are a class of water-insoluble dyes used for coloring cellulosic fibers. Acid dyes are water-soluble and mainly used for dyeing protein fibers like wool. "Others" refer to various other types of dyes used for specific applications and materials.</p></p>
