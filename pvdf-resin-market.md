<p><strong>The PVDF Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVDF Granule</li><li>PVDF Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDF Resin is available in two main types: PVDF Granule and PVDF Powder. PVDF Granule refers to the resin material that is in the form of small granules or pellets. It is commonly used for injection molding, extrusion, and other thermoplastic processing methods. On the other hand, PVDF Powder is the resin material in a powdered form. This type is typically utilized in coating applications, such as powder coating or wet coating processes. Both types of PVDF resin offer excellent chemical resistance, heat resistance, and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
