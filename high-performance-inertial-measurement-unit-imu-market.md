<p><p>The high-performance inertial measurement unit (IMU) market consists of two main types: gyroscope and accelerometers. Gyroscopes measure and monitor angular motion in three axes, allowing for accurate orientation and rotation tracking. Accelerometers, on the other hand, capture linear acceleration and vibration data in three axes. Both technologies play a crucial role in various applications, such as autonomous vehicles, aerospace, robotics, and virtual reality, where precise motion sensing and control are essential factors for high-performance operation.</p></p>
