<p><p>ICs, or Integrated Circuits, play a crucial role in the wireless charging system market. Transmitter ICs handle the power transmission from the charger to the device being charged, while receiver ICs receive the power and convert it into usable energy for the device. Transmitter ICs ensure efficient power transfer, control the charging process, and offer safety features. On the other hand, receiver ICs manage the energy received, regulating it to maintain safe charging levels. Both transmitter and receiver ICs are pivotal components in wireless charging systems, ensuring smooth and reliable power transfer.</p></p>
