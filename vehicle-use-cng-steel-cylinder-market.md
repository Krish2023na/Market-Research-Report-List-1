<p><p>The Vehicle Use CNG Steel Cylinder market is categorized into four types: CNG-1, CNG-2, CNG-3, and CNG-4. CNG-1 refers to cylinders made of seamless steel that can withstand a pressure of up to 20 MPa and are typically used for low-pressure vehicles. CNG-2 cylinders are also made of seamless steel but can handle a higher pressure of up to 25 MPa, suitable for medium-pressure vehicles. CNG-3 and CNG-4 cylinders are made of welded steel and are capable of handling pressures ranging from 25 to 30 MPa and above 30 MPa, respectively, making them suitable for high-pressure vehicles.</p></p>
