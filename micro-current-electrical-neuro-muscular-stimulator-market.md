<p><p>A Micro Current Electrical Neuro Muscular Stimulator (MCENS) is a device that delivers low-level electrical currents to stimulate the nerves and muscles in the body. MCENS devices are also used for cosmetic purposes, such as skin rejuvenation and toning.</p><p>The current outlook of the MCENS market is highly promising and set to experience steady growth in the coming years. The market is driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic pain conditions and the rising demand for non-invasive and drug-free treatment options.
