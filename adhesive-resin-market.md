<p><p>The adhesive resin market encompasses various types of adhesives, including water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, reactive, and others. Water-based adhesives are formulated with water as a solvent, offering easy application and low toxicity. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing fast drying time and excellent bond strength. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt upon heating, allowing for quick bonding. Reactive adhesives chemically react to form a strong bond. Other types of adhesives include pressure-sensitive adhesives, UV-curable adhesives, and hybrid adhesives, each with unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
