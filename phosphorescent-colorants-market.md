<p><p>The phosphorescent colorant market can be classified into two types: organic phosphorescent colorants and inorganic phosphorescent colorants. They are commonly used in industries like textile, paint, and plastic. On the other hand, inorganic phosphorescent colorants are made from non-carbon based compounds and offer superior light stability and longer phosphorescence duration. They are extensively used in applications requiring long-lasting luminescent effects, such as safety signs and emergency exit signs.</p></p>
