<p><p>The biliary stent market consists of two main types: metal stents and polymer stents. Metal stents are typically made of stainless steel or nitinol, providing excellent radial support and durability. They are used in cases where long-term stenting is needed due to their ability to remain in the biliary tract for extended periods. On the other hand, polymer stents are made of biocompatible materials like silicone or polyurethane. They are often used for short-term stenting, as they have the advantage of being easily removable and less expensive than metal stents.</p></p>
