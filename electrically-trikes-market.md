<p><p>Electrically trikes market types refer to the different types of electric trikes available in the market. Side wheel motors are a type of electric trike that have electric motors mounted on the side wheels. This configuration provides an additional propulsion force and stability to the trike. On the other hand, center motors are electric trikes that have their motors mounted in the central part of the trike, usually closer to the bottom bracket. This configuration offers a more balanced and efficient power distribution, enhancing the overall performance of the trike.</p></p>
