<p><strong>The Subsea Blowout Preventer (BOP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Annular Blowout Preventer</li><li>Ram Blowout Preventer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Subsea Blowout Preventer (BOP) market consists of two main types: Annular Blowout Preventer and Ram Blowout Preventer. Annular BOPs are designed with a rubber sealing element that securely closes around the drill pipe during drilling operations, preventing potential blowouts. On the other hand, Ram BOPs use hydraulically operated rams to seal off the wellbore, effectively controlling any unexpected outbursts. Both types are critical safety devices used in offshore drilling operations to prevent the uncontrolled release of oil or gas from the well.</p></p>
