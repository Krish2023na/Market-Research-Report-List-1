<p><p>The Epoxy Low Temperature Coatings market comprises two main types: Water Based Epoxy Low Temperature Coatings and Solvent Based Epoxy Low Temperature Coatings. Water Based coatings consist of epoxy resins that are dispersed in water, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Solvent Based coatings, on the other hand, use solvents as carriers for epoxy resins. These coatings offer faster drying times and high chemical resistance. Both types are commonly used for protecting surfaces in low temperature environments.</p></p>
