<p><strong>The Microplate Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Microplate Reader market comprises two types: Optical Filter Microplate Reader and Optical Grating Microplate Reader. Optical Filter Microplate Reader employs filters to measure specific wavelengths of light, suitable for applications where precise wavelength analysis is required. On the other hand, Optical Grating Microplate Reader uses diffraction gratings to separate light into different wavelengths, enabling a wider range of wavelengths for analysis and making it ideal for multi-wavelength measurements. Both types play crucial roles in research laboratories, pharmaceutical companies, and academic institutions for various applications like drug discovery, enzyme kinetics, and protein quantification.</p></p>
