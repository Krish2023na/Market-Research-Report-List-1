<p><p>A Magnetoresistive (MR) sensor is a type of sensor that utilizes changes in the electrical resistance of a material in the presence of a magnetic field to detect and measure magnetic fields. <p><p>The Magnetoresistive (MR) Sensor market includes various types of sensors. Thin Film Magnetoresistive Sensors are constructed using thin films of magnetic materials and offer high sensitivity. Anisotropic Magnetoresistive Sensors consist of multiple layers of magnetic and non-magnetic materials, providing enhanced sensitivity and larger signal output. Giant Magnetoresistive Sensors utilize a variable magnetic field and offer high sensitivity and low power consumption. Tunnel Magnetoresistive Sensors are based on the quantum tunneling effect and provide high sensitivity and a wide range of applications. Other types of MR sensors include spin valve sensors and magnetic field sensors.</p></p>
