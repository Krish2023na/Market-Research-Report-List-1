<p><ul><li>Crosslinked Type</li><li>Non Crosslinked Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Window insulation film is a product used to enhance energy efficiency and maintain comfortable indoor temperatures. The market for this film can be categorized into two types: crosslinked and non-crosslinked. Crosslinked window insulation film is developed by treating the material with radiation, creating a stronger and more durable film. It provides better insulation, is tear-resistant, and has a longer lifespan. On the other hand, non-crosslinked window insulation film is not treated with radiation, making it less durable and tear-resistant. It offers less insulation and has a shorter lifespan compared to the crosslinked type.</p></p>
