<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Polyurethane spray coatings market is categorized into three types: solvent-based, water-based, and other. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents like acetone or xylene as a carrier to dissolve the polyurethane components. These coatings offer excellent adhesion and resistance properties but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-based coatings, on the other hand, use water as a carrier, making them eco-friendly and low in VOCs. Other market segments include UV-cured coatings, powder coatings, and high solids coatings, which offer specific advantages such as fast curing, environmental-friendly nature, and exceptional durability, respectively.</p></p>
