<p><p>The 3D cell culture hydrogel market offers two main types of hydrogels: natural hydrogels and synthetic hydrogels. Natural hydrogels are derived from natural sources such as collagen or hyaluronic acid, providing a more biocompatible environment for cell growth. Synthetic hydrogels, on the other hand, are created in the laboratory using polymers designed to mimic the extracellular matrix. These hydrogels offer better control over their physical and chemical properties, allowing researchers to tailor them for specific applications in tissue engineering and drug discovery.</p></p>
