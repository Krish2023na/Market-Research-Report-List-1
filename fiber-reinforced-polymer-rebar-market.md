<p><p>The Fiber Reinforced Polymer (FRP) rebar market includes various types of reinforcing materials such as Glass Fiber, Amide Fiber, Basalt Fiber, and Carbon Fiber systems. Glass Fiber rebar consists of glass fibers embedded in a polymer matrix, offering a cost-effective and corrosion-resistant solution. Amide Fiber rebar uses aramid fibers with high tensile strength and lightweight properties, suitable for applications requiring high performance. Basalt Fiber system utilizes basalt fibers known for their excellent resistance to acids, alkalis, and UV rays. Carbon Fiber system employs carbon fibers that provide high strength, durability, and stiffness, making it ideal for specialized construction projects.</p></p>
