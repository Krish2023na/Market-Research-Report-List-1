<p><p>Commodity plastics are types of plastics that are widely used in various industries due to their low cost and versatile properties. These plastics are extensively used in construction, household goods, and automotive parts, which are essential for the development of infrastructure and urbanization.</p><p>However, there are some challenges that the commodity plastic market may face in the future.
