<p><p>The Back Glue (BG) market is divided into three main types: 1 Component Paste BG, 1 Component Liquid BG, and 2 Component BG. 1 Component Paste BG is a thick, adhesive substance that is directly used for gluing. 1 Component Liquid BG, on the other hand, is a more fluid form of glue that can be easily applied using a solvent or applicator. Lastly, 2 Component BG consists of two separate chemical components that must be mixed together before application, resulting in a strong and durable bond. Each type of BG offers different application methods and bonding qualities, catering to various industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
