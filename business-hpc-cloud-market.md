<p><p>The Business HPC (High-Performance Computing) Cloud market comprises three main types: Public Clouds, Private Clouds, and Hybrid Clouds. Public Clouds refer to HPC resources provided by third-party cloud service providers accessible to multiple organizations over the internet. Private Clouds involve HPC infrastructure owned and managed by a single organization for exclusive use. Hybrid Clouds combine the benefits of both Public and Private Clouds, allowing organizations to allocate HPC workloads across multiple environments based on their specific needs, while maintaining control over sensitive data.</p></p>
