<p><p>Tricuspid valve interventional therapy refers to various medical procedures used to treat tricuspid valve disorders. Leaflet clamping involves using specialized tools to temporarily immobilize the valve's leaflets, allowing for repairs or replacements. Annulus retraction entails retracting or reducing the size of the valve's annulus to improve its function. Increasing the matching area involves enhancing the valve's fit by modifying or expanding the available space. These techniques aim to address tricuspid valve abnormalities, ultimately improving patient outcomes and cardiac function.</p></p>
