<p><p>Desiccant inserters are devices used to insert desiccant packets into products or packaging to maintain their optimal moisture levels. Canister desiccant inserters commonly use a cylindrical container filled with desiccant beads, which can be easily integrated into automated packaging lines. On the other hand, pouch desiccant inserters use flexible pouches filled with desiccant materials that can be inserted manually into products. Both types offer efficient moisture control solutions, with canisters suitable for large-scale production and pouches for smaller operations or when manual insertion is preferred.</p></p>
