<p><strong>The Membrane Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography</li><li>Affinity Film Chromatography</li><li>Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane chromatography refers to a technique used for protein purification in biopharmaceutical production. Ion exchange membrane chromatography involves the separation of proteins based on their charge properties. Affinity film chromatography utilizes specific interactions between proteins and ligands immobilized on the membrane. Hydrophobic interaction membrane chromatography relies on hydrophobic interactions between proteins and the membrane surface. These different types of membrane chromatography offer diverse purification capabilities, resulting in more efficient and precise protein separation processes in the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
