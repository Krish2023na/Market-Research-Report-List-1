<p><p>Cleanroom packaging refers to the process of packaging products in a controlled environment to prevent any contamination from external sources. This packaging is commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, medical devices, aerospace, and electronics, where hygiene and safety are paramount.</p><p>Cleanroom packaging ensures that the products are protected from dust particles, airborne microbes, humidity, and other contaminants. The packaging materials used in cleanroom packaging are specially designed and manufactured to meet the stringent requirements of the controlled environment. These materials include films, bags, pouches, trays, and containers that have low particulate and microbial content.</p><p>The cleanroom packaging market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for cleanroom packaging in the pharmaceutical and healthcare sectors due to the rising emphasis on maintaining product quality and safety is one of the key factors driving market growth. Additionally, the growing use of cleanroom packaging in the electronics industry to protect sensitive components and devices from contamination is also fueling market expansion.</p><p>Furthermore, the implementation of strict regulations and guidelines by regulatory authorities regarding product packaging and safety is boosting the demand for cleanroom packaging solutions.
