<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The microelectronic cleaning equipment market consists of four main types of equipment. Single-wafer cryogenic systems use ultra-cold temperatures to remove contaminants from individual wafers. Single-wafer spray systems use a high-pressure spray to clean individual wafers. Batch immersion cleaning systems can clean multiple wafers simultaneously by immersing them in a cleaning solution. Batch spray cleaning systems clean multiple wafers by subjecting them to a spray of cleaning solution. These different types of equipment cater to specific cleaning needs in the microelectronics industry.</p></p>
