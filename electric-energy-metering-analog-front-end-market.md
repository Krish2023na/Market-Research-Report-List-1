<p><ul><li>Simplex</li><li>Polyphase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric energy metering analog front end market types can be classified into Simplex and Polyphase. Simplex meters are single-phase meters that measure electricity consumption in residential homes, small businesses, and single-phase loads. These meters typically have a single channel for measuring current and voltage. On the other hand, Polyphase meters are used in industrial and commercial settings where three-phase loads are common. They have multiple channels for measuring currents and voltages in each phase, providing accurate measurements and billing for complex power distribution systems.</p></p>
