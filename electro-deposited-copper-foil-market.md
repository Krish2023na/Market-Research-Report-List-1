Ultra-thin Copper Foil is often used in advanced electronic devices, flexible printed circuits, and batteries, where space and weight constraints are critical. General Copper Foil, with its higher thickness, is commonly employed in applications like printed circuit boards and electromagnetic shielding, where mechanical strength and durability are crucial.</p></p>
