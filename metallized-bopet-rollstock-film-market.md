It is manufactured by depositing a thin layer of metal (usually aluminum) onto one side of the BOPET film through a vacuum deposition process.</p><p>The metallized BOPET rollstock film market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for flexible packaging solutions in various end-use industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, personal care, and electronics is driving the market growth. This film offers excellent oxygen and moisture barrier properties, which helps in preserving the freshness and extending the shelf life of products. It also provides a glossy appearance and enhances the visual appeal of the packaging, making it more attractive to consumers.</p><p>Moreover, the growing preference for sustainable and eco-friendly packaging materials is further fueling the demand for metallized BOPET rollstock films.
