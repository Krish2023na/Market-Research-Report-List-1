<p><p>The Compact Camera Module (CCM) market is categorized into two main types: CCD and CMOS. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) is a sensor technology that converts light into electrical charge and offers high-quality image output. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) is a more modern and widely used sensor technology that provides better power efficiency and faster processing speed. Both CCD and CMOS contribute to the growth of the CCM market by offering different benefits and catering to diverse customer requirements in terms of image quality, power consumption, and performance.</p></p>
