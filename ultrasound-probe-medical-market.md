<p><p>The ultrasound probe medical market encompasses several types of probes used in various medical applications. The linear type probe is primarily used for imaging superficial structures with high resolution. The convex type probe has a wider field of view, making it suitable for abdominal and obstetric imaging. The phased array type probe is used for cardiac and vascular imaging due to its ability to produce real-time images. Endocavitary probes are designed for gynecological and urological examinations. Lastly, there are other specialized probes catering to specific medical procedures such as biopsies or intraoperative imaging.</p></p>
