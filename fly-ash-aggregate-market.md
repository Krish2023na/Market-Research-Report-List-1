<p><p>Fly ash aggregate is a lightweight construction material made from the combustion by-products of coal-fired power plants, primarily fly ash. It is produced by combining fly ash with cement and water to form a solid aggregate that is then cured and hardened. Fly ash aggregate offers several advantages over traditional aggregates, including reduced weight, improved thermal insulation, and enhanced fire resistance.</p><p>The future outlook for the fly ash aggregate market is promising. The growing focus on sustainable construction practices and the increasing need for lightweight and eco-friendly building materials are driving the demand for fly ash aggregate. Additionally, the rising awareness about the environmental hazards associated with the disposal of fly ash has led to the adoption of fly ash aggregates as a suitable solution for its utilization.</p><p>The current market for fly ash aggregate is also witnessing significant growth.
