<p><p>OSB (oriented strand board) panel market types are differentiated by their structural capabilities and intended applications. OSB/1 panels are basic, non-structural panels suitable for interior use. OSB/2 panels are designed for structural applications in dry conditions. OSB/3 panels feature improved strength and moisture resistance, making them suitable for use in humid environments. OSB/4 panels are the strongest and most durable, often used for structural applications in areas with high humidity or external exposure. These different market types ensure that OSB panels are suitable for a wide range of construction purposes.</p></p>
