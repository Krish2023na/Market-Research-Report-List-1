<p><strong>The Refractory PU Sandwich Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Metal Surface</li><li>Bimetallic Surface</li><li>Non-metallic Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory PU sandwich board market includes three types: single metal surface, bimetallic surface, and non-metallic surface. Single metal surface boards are made of a single metal layer on the surface, providing good strength and durability. Bimetallic surface boards consist of two different metal layers on the surface, offering enhanced properties such as corrosion resistance. Non-metallic surface boards, on the other hand, are made of non-metallic materials like PVC or fiberglass, which provide insulation and fire resistance. These different types cater to various needs in industries like construction and automotive.</p></p>
