Two common types of this market are Alkaline Water Electrolysis and Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolysis. In Alkaline Water Electrolysis, an alkaline electrolyte is used as the medium, while in PEM Electrolysis, a solid polymer membrane separates the electrodes.
