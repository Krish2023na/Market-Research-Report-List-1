<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The level monitoring sensor market includes various types of sensors used to measure fluid and bulk material levels. Float & tape gauging is a traditional method that uses a float attached to a tape to measure level. Pressure monitoring sensors measure levels by detecting pressure in a closed container. Ultrasonic monitoring sensors use sound waves to determine level. Capacitance monitoring sensors measure the level using changes in electrical capacitance. Radar-based monitoring sensors use electromagnetic waves to measure level. Other types include optical, nuclear, and thermal sensors.</p></p>
