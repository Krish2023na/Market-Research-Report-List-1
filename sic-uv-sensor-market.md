<p><p>The SiC UV sensor market consists of three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors are designed to detect ultraviolet light in the range of 315-400nm, commonly used in UV curing applications and tanning beds. UVB sensors, on the other hand, can detect light in the range of 280-315nm and are primarily used in UV index monitoring and skin cancer prevention. UVC sensors detect ultraviolet light in the range of 100-280nm, and they find applications in sterilization, water purification, and germicidal lamps.</p></p>
