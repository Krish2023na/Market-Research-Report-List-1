<p><p>Food antimicrobials and antioxidants are substances added to food products to prevent or inhibit the growth of microorganisms and to prevent oxidation, respectively. Antioxidants, on the other hand, prevent or slow down the deterioration of food caused by oxidation reactions, which can lead to rancidity and discoloration.</p><p>The market for food antimicrobials and antioxidants is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Moreover, the development of innovative antimicrobial and antioxidant solutions and the implementation of strict food safety regulations by governing bodies are further propelling market growth.</p><p>Overall, the future outlook for the food antimicrobial and antioxidants market seems promising, with steady growth expected due to the increasing demand for safe and high-quality food products.
