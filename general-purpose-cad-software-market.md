<p><p>The general-purpose CAD software market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CAD software is accessible through the internet and is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and work on projects from any device with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises CAD software is installed and maintained locally on the user's computer or server, requiring software updates and maintenance to be done on-site. Both options offer different advantages and considerations in terms of security, accessibility, and management of CAD software.</p></p>
