<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Broiler ventilation systems are divided into different types based on their design and functionality. The ridge ventilation system is installed along the roof ridge, allowing hot air to escape and promoting air circulation. The length ventilation system is installed on the sidewalls of the broiler house, providing air exchange throughout the length of the building. Tunnel ventilation system involves fans and inlets placed at opposite ends of the building, creating a wind tunnel effect. Other types may include floor ventilation systems or combinations of the aforementioned types, allowing flexibility in meeting specific broiler house requirements.</p></p>
