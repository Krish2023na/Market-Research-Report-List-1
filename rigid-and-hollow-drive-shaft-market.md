<p><p>A rigid drive shaft is a solid shaft that is used to transmit torque from one point to another in a mechanical system. It is typically made of a strong material such as steel. The hollow design helps to reduce weight without compromising strength and stiffness.</p><p>The future of the rigid and hollow drive shaft market is expected to be positive. The increasing demand for lightweight and fuel-efficient vehicles is driving the growth of this market.
