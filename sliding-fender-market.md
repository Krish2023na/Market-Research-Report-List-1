<p><p>A sliding fender is a type of marine fender system that is designed to handle the forces exerted during the berthing and mooring of ships and boats. It consists of a series of rubber panels that are interconnected and mounted on horizontal rails. These panels slide along the rails to distribute the energy generated upon impact, minimizing the damage caused to both the ship and the pier.</p><p>The sliding fender market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
