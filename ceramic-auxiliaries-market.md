<p><p>Ceramic auxiliaries refer to various additives used in ceramic manufacturing processes. The market types of ceramic auxiliaries include rheology modifiers, defoamers, dispersants, wetting agents, and others. Rheology modifiers are responsible for controlling the flow and viscosity of ceramic suspensions. Defoamers are used to eliminate foam formation during the processing of ceramic materials. Dispersants help in evenly dispersing the particles in the ceramic suspension. Wetting agents improve the wetting properties of ceramic materials, facilitating their mixing with water or other liquids. Other types of ceramic auxiliaries include binders, plasticizers, lubricants, and anti-settling agents.</p></p>
