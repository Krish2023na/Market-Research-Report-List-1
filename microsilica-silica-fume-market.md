Microsilica is primarily used as a concrete additive to enhance the strength, durability, and performance of concrete structures.</p><p>The current outlook for the microsilica market is positive and is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years. The global construction industry is a major driver for the demand of microsilica, as it is widely used in high-performance concrete applications.
