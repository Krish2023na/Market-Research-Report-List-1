<p><p>The dental ceramic binder market offers different types of adhesives, namely self-etching adhesive, selective-etching adhesive, and total-etching adhesive. Self-etching adhesive is a type of dental adhesive that combines etching and priming steps into a single process, simplifying application. Selective-etching adhesive selectively etches specific areas of the tooth surface before applying the adhesive. Total-etching adhesive involves etching the entire tooth surface before adhesive application. These different types of dental adhesives cater to various preferences and requirements of dental professionals, providing options for effective and reliable bonding of dental ceramics.</p></p>
