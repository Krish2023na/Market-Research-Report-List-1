<p><p>A vacuum annealing furnace is a type of heat treatment equipment used for annealing various metal and alloy materials in a vacuum environment. Annealing is a process that involves heating materials to a specific temperature and then slowly cooling them to alter their physical and mechanical properties, such as improving their ductility, toughness, and reducing their internal stress.</p><p>The vacuum annealing furnace creates a vacuum environment to protect the materials from oxidation and other adverse reactions that can occur in an air atmosphere. This process ensures that the materials undergo a clean and controlled heat treatment, resulting in improved product quality.</p><p>The global vacuum annealing furnace market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
