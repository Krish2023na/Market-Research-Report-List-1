<p><p>Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft refer to electric-powered aircraft that can take off and land vertically. These aircraft are categorized into three market types. Tilt-X eVTOLs have tilting wings or rotors that transition between vertical take-off and forward flight. Lift+Cruise eVTOLs make use of fixed wings to generate lift during forward flight, in addition to vertical take-off and landing capabilities. Multi-copters are eVTOLs equipped with multiple rotors for lifting and propulsion. These different market types offer various advantages and applications within the eVTOL industry.</p></p>
