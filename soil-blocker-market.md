<p><p>A soil blocker is a gardening tool used to create seedling blocks or soil cubes for starting plants from seeds. It is designed to compress soil or potting mix into rectangular, cube-shaped blocks that hold the perfect amount of moisture and air for plant growth. These blocks are then used as individual containers for seedlings, eliminating the need for traditional plastic pots. The main advantages of using a soil blocker are reduced transplant shock, healthier root system development, and improved overall plant growth.</p><p>The current outlook of the soil blocker market is positive and is expected to grow significantly in the coming years.
