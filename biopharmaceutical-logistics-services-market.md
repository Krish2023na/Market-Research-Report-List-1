<p><ul><li>Cold Chain Logistics</li><li>Non-cold Chain Logistics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biopharmaceutical logistics services market can be divided into two major types: cold chain logistics and non-cold chain logistics. Cold chain logistics involves the transportation and storage of biopharmaceutical products under specific temperature conditions to maintain their efficacy. This is crucial for sensitive products like vaccines and biological drugs. On the other hand, non-cold chain logistics refers to the transportation and storage of biopharmaceutical products that do not require specific temperature control. Both types are important components of the biopharmaceutical supply chain, ensuring the safe and efficient delivery of products to end users.</p></p>
