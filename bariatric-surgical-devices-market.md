<p><strong>The Bariatric Surgical Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Assisting Devices</li><li>Implantable Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bariatric Surgical Devices market is categorized into two types: Assisting Devices and Implantable Devices. Assisting Devices refer to external devices used during bariatric surgery to aid the procedure, such as stapling devices, trocars, and suturing devices. On the other hand, Implantable Devices are permanent devices placed inside the body to facilitate weight loss or control, like gastric bands, electrical stimulation systems, and gastric balloons. Both types of devices play a crucial role in bariatric surgery, assisting in the treatment of obesity and promoting long-term weight loss.</p></p>
