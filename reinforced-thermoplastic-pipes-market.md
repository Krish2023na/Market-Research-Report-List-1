<p><p>Reinforced Thermoplastic Pipes (RTP) market consists of two main types: Non-metallic RTP and Metallic RTP. Non-metallic RTPs are composed of layers of polymer materials, such as high-density polyethylene or nylon, reinforced with fiber reinforcements. They are commonly used in applications where high resistance to corrosion and chemical durability is required. On the other hand, Metallic RTPs are reinforced with layers of metal, such as steel or aluminum. They offer high strength and stiffness, making them suitable for applications that require high pressure and extreme temperature resistance.</p></p>
