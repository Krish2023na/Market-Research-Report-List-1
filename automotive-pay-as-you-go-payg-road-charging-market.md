<p><p>The Automotive Pay As You Go (PAYG) Road Charging market consists of different types, such as Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) and All Electronic Toll Collection (AETC). ETC is a method where tolls are collected automatically by electronic means, usually through a transponder attached to the vehicle. AETC, on the other hand, refers to a comprehensive system where all tolls are collected electronically, without the option of manual payment. Both these market types aim to provide convenience to drivers by eliminating the need for physical payment at toll booths.</p></p>
