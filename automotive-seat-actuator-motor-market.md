<p><p>The automotive seat actuator motor market consists of two main types: two-pole motors and four-pole motors. Two-pole motors are characterized by having two magnetic poles, resulting in higher rotational speeds and lower torque. They are commonly used in applications where speed is a priority, such as power seat adjustments. On the other hand, four-pole motors have four magnetic poles and offer higher torque with lower rotational speeds. These motors are generally used in applications requiring higher force, like seat reclining and tilting mechanisms.</p></p>
