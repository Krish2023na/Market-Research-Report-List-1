The rise in data breaches, identity theft, and unauthorized access attempts has led to a greater focus on secure authentication methods. OTP hardware authentication offers a higher level of protection as OTPs are time-limited and can only be used once, making them difficult for hackers to exploit.</p><p>Organizations across various sectors, including banking, financial services, healthcare, government, and IT, are adopting OTP hardware authentication solutions to safeguard sensitive information and prevent unauthorized access.
