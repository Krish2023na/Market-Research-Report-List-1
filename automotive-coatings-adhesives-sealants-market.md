<p><p>Automotive coatings, adhesives, and sealants are essential for enhancing vehicle durability, safety, and appearance. Solvent-based coatings are typically composed of organic solvents that provide excellent surface coverage and are resistant to environmental conditions. However, they emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), which can pose environmental and health risks. Water-based coatings, on the other hand, use water as a carrier instead of solvents, resulting in lower VOC emissions. Although water-based coatings may require multiple layers and longer drying times, they offer better sustainability and safety profiles compared to solvent-based coatings.</p></p>
