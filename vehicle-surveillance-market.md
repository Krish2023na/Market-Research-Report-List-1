<p><p>Blind Spot Detection System (BSDS) is a type of vehicle surveillance technology that uses sensors to detect objects in a driver's blind spots, alerting them to potential dangers. Autonomous Cruise Control System (ACCS) is a market type that enables vehicles to automatically adjust their speed and maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead. Parking Assist System (PAS) helps drivers park their vehicles by providing visual or audio cues. Lane Departure Warning System (LDW) alerts drivers when they unintentionally drift out of their lane. Heads-Up Display Device (HUDD) projects important information onto the windshield, allowing drivers to keep their eyes on the road. Global Positioning System (GPS) market type provides real-time positioning and navigation information to drivers.</p></p>
