<p><p>Epoxy bonding agents are commonly used in construction and other industries to bond different materials together. They are available in three main types based on their viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity epoxy bonding agents have a thin consistency and are ideal for applications where quick penetration and large surface coverage is needed. Medium viscosity epoxies offer a balance between penetration and gap filling properties. High viscosity epoxies are thicker and are primarily used for filling larger gaps and voids in materials.</p></p>
