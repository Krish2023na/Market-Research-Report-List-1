<p><ul><li>Cross-belt Sorters</li><li>Tilt tray Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unit sorters are automated systems used to sort various items accurately and efficiently. Cross-belt sorters utilize a conveyor belt with mini belts perpendicular to the main belt to transport items to their designated destinations. This system boasts high sorting speeds and can handle a wide range of product sizes. On the other hand, tilt tray sorters utilize trays that tilt to release items into chutes, allowing for gentle handling of fragile items. Tilt tray sorters are versatile, flexible, and suitable for a variety of industries. Overall, both types of sorters offer efficient and precise sorting solutions for different needs.</p></p>
