<p><strong>The Honeycomb Paperboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Paper Honeycomb</li><li>Blocks Paper Honeycomb</li><li>Expanded Paper Honeycomb</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Honeycomb paperboard is categorized into three main types - continuous paper honeycomb, blocks paper honeycomb, and expanded paper honeycomb. Continuous paper honeycomb is made by continuously winding paper onto a core, providing strength and durability. Blocks paper honeycomb is created by slicing paper vertically and then joining the layers, resulting in higher strength and resilience. Expanded paper honeycomb is constructed by expanding kraft paper, producing a lighter and more cost-effective solution. These variations serve different purposes, offering unique benefits in packaging, furniture, construction, automotive, and other industries.</p></p>
