<p><p>Solid masterbatches are used in the plastic industry as additives to enhance the properties of plastic materials. White masterbatch is used to provide opacity, whiteness, and brightness to plastics. Black masterbatch is employed to achieve black color in plastics. Color masterbatch is used to produce various colors in plastic materials. Additive masterbatch is used to incorporate specific properties like UV resistance or flame retardancy into plastics. Plastic filler masterbatch is used to add fillers like calcium carbonate or talc to improve the strength and reduce the cost of plastic materials.</p></p>
