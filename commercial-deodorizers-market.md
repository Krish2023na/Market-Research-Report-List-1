<p><p>In the commercial deodorizers market, there are three main types of products available. Physical type deodorizers include devices or products that physically absorb or mask unpleasant odors, such as air fresheners or odor-eliminating sprays. Chemical type deodorizers consist of products that use chemicals to neutralize or eliminate odors, such as deodorizing agents or odor control chemicals. Lastly, microbial type deodorizers utilize beneficial microbes that break down organic matter and eliminate odors, commonly found in products like bio-enzymatic cleaners or waste treatment solutions. These different types cater to various odor elimination needs in commercial settings.</p></p>
