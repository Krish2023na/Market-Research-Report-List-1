<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) lamps are available in two main types - High Boron Glass Lamp and Quartz Glass Lamp. High Boron Glass Lamps are made of a borosilicate glass, providing excellent UV transmission and durability. These lamps are ideal for applications that require deep UV radiation. On the other hand, Quartz Glass Lamps are made of pure quartz, ensuring maximum UV radiation output. They have superior resistance to temperature and chemical corrosion, making them suitable for various industrial and laboratory applications that require intense UV radiation.</p></p>
