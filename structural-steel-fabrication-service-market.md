<p><p>The market for structural steel fabrication services can be categorized into different types based on the shapes of steel used in construction. These include I-Beam, which provides strength and structural support; Angle (L-Shape), widely used for framing and corner reinforcements; Hollow Structural Section (HSS) Shape, used for its lightweight and load-bearing capabilities; Z-Shape, offering increased torsional strength; and T-Shaped, known for its stability and use in foundations. Each of these shapes serves various construction purposes, catering to different structural design requirements in the market.</p></p>
