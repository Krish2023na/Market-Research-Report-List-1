<p><p>Hydrogenated Diamond-Like Carbon Coating (DLC) is a type of protective coating with exceptional hardness, low friction, and high resistance to wear. It finds applications in various industries. The market for DLC coatings is categorized into three types: PVD, PECVD, and others. PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) involves the condensation of vapor onto a substrate. PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) uses a chemical reaction in a plasma to deposit the coating. The "others" category comprises alternative deposition methods such as cathodic arc, ion beam, or laser-assisted techniques. These different market types offer specific advantages and are employed based on the desired coating properties and production requirements.</p></p>
