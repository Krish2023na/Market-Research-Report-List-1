<p><p>Pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH) equipment refers to medical devices and equipment used for the diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment of pulmonary arterial hypertension. Technological advancements in the field of medical devices, such as portable and non-invasive monitoring devices and advanced imaging systems, have improved the diagnosis and monitoring of PAH patients. Additionally, the development of targeted therapies and continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) devices has revolutionized the treatment of PAH, leading to better patient outcomes.</p><p>The future outlook of the PAH equipment market is expected to remain promising.
