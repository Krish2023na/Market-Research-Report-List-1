<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Infection Prevention Devices market?</strong></p>
<p><p>Emerging trends in the global infection prevention devices market include the rising adoption of disposable and single-use devices to prevent cross-contamination, the increasing focus on antimicrobial coatings to reduce device-associated infections, and the growing utilization of advanced technologies like ultraviolet (UV) light sterilization and microbicidal electrostatic sprays. The market is witnessing a shift towards prevention rather than treatment, leading to the development of innovative solutions such as wearable infection prevention devices and point-of-care disinfection devices.
