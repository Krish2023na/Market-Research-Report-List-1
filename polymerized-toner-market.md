<p><p>Polymerized toner is a type of toner used in industries for printing purposes. It is classified into two categories - positive polymerized toner and passive polymerized toner. Positive polymerized toner is a technology that uses positive charging particles to form the images on the paper during the printing process. On the other hand, passive polymerized toner relies on negative charging particles. Both types of polymerized toners offer enhanced image quality, durability, and environmental friendliness, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
