<p><p>Medical cautery pens are used in a range of medical procedures for heat-based treatments, such as the removal of skin lesions or stopping bleeding. They come in two types: hand control and foot control. Hand control cautery pens require the user to manually activate the device by pressing a button or trigger, allowing for precise and direct control. In contrast, foot control cautery pens are operated by foot pedals, leaving the user's hands free to perform other tasks while controlling the intensity of the heat.</p></p>
