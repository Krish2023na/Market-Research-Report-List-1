Polyester film, on the other hand, provides good thermal resistance, electrical insulation, and chemical resistance. Lastly, the "others" category includes various types of thermal barrier films that are not classified under polypropylene or polyester, such as parchment paper or ceramic coatings. These films play a crucial role in preventing heat transfer and improving the efficiency and lifespan of batteries.</p></p>
