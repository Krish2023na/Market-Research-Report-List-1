<p><p>Anti-static shoes, also known as electrostatic discharge (ESD) shoes, are specially designed footwear that helps to prevent the build-up of static electricity on the body and discharge it safely to the ground. They are commonly used in industries where static electricity poses a risk to sensitive electronic equipment or in environments where flammable materials are present. These shoes primarily consist of conductive materials, such as leather or rubber, and feature anti-static soles that provide electrical conductivity.</p><p>The global anti-static shoes market is expected to witness steady growth over the forecast period.
