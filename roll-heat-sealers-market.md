<p><p>Roll heat sealers are used to create airtight seals on packaging materials. There are two main types of roll heat sealers: manual and automated. Manual roll heat sealers require human intervention to operate, where the user manually feeds the packaging material into the machine, applies heat to seal it, and then releases the sealed package. Automated roll heat sealers, on the other hand, can perform these functions automatically and continuously without the need for human intervention. These machines are typically faster and more efficient in high-volume production environments.</p></p>
