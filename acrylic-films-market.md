<p><p>The global acrylic films market can be divided into two main types: soft type and hard type. Soft type acrylic films are flexible and offer excellent impact resistance, making them ideal for applications such as packaging, labeling, and decorative purposes. On the other hand, hard type acrylic films are rigid and have high transparency, making them suitable for uses like displays, windows, and protective coatings. Both types have their unique attributes and are used in various industries, depending on the desired characteristics and requirements of the applications.</p></p>
