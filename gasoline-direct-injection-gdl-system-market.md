<p><p>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) is a fuel injection system where gasoline is directly injected into the combustion chamber of an internal combustion engine. There are different types of GDI systems. Turbulent Jet (T) system uses a high-pressure fuel injector to create a turbulent fuel-air mixture. Piezo Fuel Injectors use a piezoelectric element to control the fuel injection process precisely. Water Jet System utilizes a water injector to cool the combustion chamber, reducing the risk of engine knock. These GDI systems enhance fuel efficiency, increase power output, and decrease emissions in vehicles.</p></p>
