<p><p>Solventless resins are a type of reactive adhesive or coating that do not require the addition of solvents in their formulation. There are three types of solventless resin markets - single component, two components, and three components. Single component solventless resins are ready-to-use and do not require mixing with other components before application. Two-component solventless resins consist of two parts that need to be mixed prior to application. Three-component solventless resins require the blending of three separate components before use. These different types offer varying levels of convenience and control for different application needs.</p></p>
