<p><p>Optical Patterned Wafer Inspection Equipment (OPWIE) is a type of equipment used in the semiconductor industry to inspect wafers for defects. Bright-Field Inspection Systems use a white light source to illuminate the wafer, making it easy to detect defects on the surface. Dark-Field Inspection Systems, on the other hand, use oblique lighting to highlight defects that may be difficult to see under normal lighting conditions. Electron Beam Inspection Systems use a focused electron beam to scan the wafer and detect defects at a microscopic level. These systems are essential for ensuring the quality of semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
