<p><ul><li>Roller Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Shoe type Peristaltic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peristaltic pumps, such as the roller and shoe type, are commonly used in chemical processing. Roller peristaltic pumps contain rotating rollers that compress the flexible tube, creating a peristaltic action to move fluids. They are suitable for applications requiring high flow rates and gentle handling of fluids. On the other hand, shoe type peristaltic pumps have shoes that press the flexible tube against a rigid wall, providing higher pressure and flow capabilities. They are commonly used for viscous or abrasive fluids and in applications requiring accurate dosing or metering.</p></p>
