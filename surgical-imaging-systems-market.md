<p><p>Surgical Imaging Systems refers to the equipment used in medical procedures to obtain real-time images of the patient's body. Image intensifier C-arms are a type of surgical imaging system that use a fluorescent screen to convert X-ray images into visible light, providing high-resolution images. On the other hand, Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms) use a flat panel detector to directly capture X-ray images, offering improved image quality and faster imaging times. Both types of systems are widely used for various surgical procedures, assisting physicians in accurate diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
