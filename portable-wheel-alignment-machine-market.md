<p><p>The portable wheel alignment machine market consists of two main types: CCD wheel alignment machines and 3D wheel alignment machines. CCD wheel alignment machines use a camera-based system to measure and align the wheels of vehicles, offering accurate results. On the other hand, 3D wheel alignment machines use advanced sensor technology to capture detailed three-dimensional wheel data, providing precise alignments. Both types of machines are portable, allowing for easy transportation and usage in various locations, making them popular choices among automotive service providers.</p></p>
