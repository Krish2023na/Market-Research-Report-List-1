<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emulsion PVC Paste Resin market can be classified into two types: Micro Suspension Method and Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves the use of organic solvents to dissolve PVC resin and then applying heat and pressure to create a paste. This method produces a high-quality resin suitable for applications requiring high transparency and low viscosity. On the other hand, the Emulsion Method involves dispersing PVC resin particles in water and using emulsifiers and stabilizers to create a stable emulsion. This method is more cost-effective and commonly used for various applications in the market.</p></p>
