These resins are widely employed in industries where maintaining the freshness and quality of products is crucial.</p><p>On the other hand, PVDC Latex is a water-based emulsion of PVDC resins. It is predominantly used in the manufacturing of flexible packaging films, where it provides enhanced clarity, adhesion, printability, and barrier properties. PVDC latex finds extensive usage in industries such as food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and electronics.</p></p>
