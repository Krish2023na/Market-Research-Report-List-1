<p><p>The aircraft brake caliper market consists of three main types: single piston brake calipers, double piston brake calipers, and multiple piston brake calipers. Single piston brake caliper type includes a single piston that applies force on the brake rotor. Double piston brake caliper type utilizes two pistons for even distribution of force. Multiple piston brake caliper type utilizes three or more pistons to enhance braking performance. Each type offers unique features and advantages, catering to the specific requirements and preferences of aircraft manufacturers and operators.</p></p>
