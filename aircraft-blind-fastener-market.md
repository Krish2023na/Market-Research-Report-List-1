<p><p>The Aircraft Blind Fastener Market comprises two main types: Drive-Pin and Break-Stem fasteners. Drive-Pin fasteners are designed to join two or more materials by driving a pin through the fastener body, resulting in a secure connection. On the other hand, Break-Stem fasteners involve a stem that is pulled through the fastener body, causing the stem to break off once secured. Both types offer efficient and reliable solutions for fastening aircraft components, ensuring structural integrity and streamlined manufacturing processes.</p></p>
