<p><p>The Security Camera Lens market encompasses three distinct types: fixed lens, varifocal lens, and zoom lens. A fixed lens comes with a specific focal length permanently set, providing a fixed field of view. Varifocal lens allows users to manually adjust the focal length, offering a customizable field of view. On the other hand, zoom lens offers both remotely adjustable focal length and field of view, ideal for surveillance purposes. These lens types cater to different security camera requirements, allowing users to choose the appropriate lens based on their specific needs.</p></p>
