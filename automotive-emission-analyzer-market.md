<p><p>The Automotive Emission Analyzer market consists of various types of analyzers used to measure and analyze emissions from vehicles. Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers use infrared light to detect and quantify gases like CO and CO2. Flame Ionization Detector (FID) measures hydrocarbon emissions by ionizing and detecting their combustion products. Chemiluminescence Analyzers (CLA) measure nitrogen oxides by utilizing a chemical reaction that produces light. Constant Volume Samplers (CVS) collect and analyze exhaust samples in a controlled environment. There are also other types of analyzers available that cater to specific emission measurement requirements.</p></p>
