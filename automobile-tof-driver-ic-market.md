<p><p>The Direct Time-of-Flight (dToF) Driver IC market refers to the segment of the automobile industry that deals with driver assistance systems based on direct ToF technology. These systems use laser or infrared sensors to measure the distance between the vehicle and obstacles, providing accurate real-time data for applications like autonomous driving and parking assistance.</p><p>On the other hand, the Indirect Time-of-Flight (dToF) Driver IC market refers to the segment that utilizes indirect ToF technology. Instead of measuring distances through direct reflection, this technology calculates distances based on the time taken for light signals to reach various points and then return to the sensor. It is commonly used for gesture recognition, monitoring driver fatigue, and occupant detection in automobiles.</p></p>
