<p><p>Additives for inkjet ink are used to enhance performance and obtain desired characteristics. Dispersing agents help in the even dispersion of pigments and prevent clumping. Leveling agents ensure an even and smooth ink coating by reducing surface tension. Defoamers eliminate bubbles and foam that can affect print quality. Emulsifiers assist in the emulsification of water and oil-based components. Corrosion inhibitors prevent inkjet heads from corroding over time. Other additives include viscosity modifiers, anti-blocking agents, and anti-oxidants, which increase ink stability and prevent clogging or drying.</p></p>
