Aftermarket, on the other hand, refers to the market where hubcaps are purchased and installed by vehicle owners or third-party suppliers, rather than being provided by the original manufacturer. Both these markets cater to the demand for hubcaps in the automotive industry, but the OEM market focuses on supplying hubcaps directly to vehicle manufacturers, while the Aftermarket market caters to individual vehicle owners and different suppliers.</p></p>
