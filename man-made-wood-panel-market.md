OSB is created by compressing wood strands with resin, while MDF/HDF is manufactured from wood fibers using high pressure and glue. Hardboard is a dense fiberboard made from wood fibers and other materials. The "others" category includes various specialty wood panels made for specific purposes.</p></p>
