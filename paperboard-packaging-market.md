<p><p>Paperboard packaging market includes various types such as chipboard and laminated/pasted chipboard. Chipboard, also known as cardboard, is a thick and rigid paper-based material used for packaging applications. It is commonly used in industries like food, beverages, electronics, and cosmetics. On the other hand, laminated/pasted chipboard consists of multiple layers of chipboard sheets that are combined and bonded together. This type of packaging offers increased strength and durability, making it ideal for heavy or fragile products. Both chipboard and laminated/pasted chipboard are widely used in the packaging industry due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
