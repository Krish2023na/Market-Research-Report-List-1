<p><p>The Rubber Accelerator Masterbatch market is categorized into three types: EPDM-based, NBR-based, and Others. EPDM-based masterbatch is made from ethylene-propylene-diene monomer rubber and is known for its excellent weather resistance, electrical insulation properties, and low-temperature flexibility. NBR-based masterbatch is made from nitrile butadiene rubber and offers good oil resistance, high tensile strength, and heat resistance. The Others category includes different types of rubber-based masterbatches not falling under EPDM or NBR, each with its specific properties and applications. These categorizations help to differentiate and select the appropriate rubber accelerator masterbatch based on specific needs.</p></p>
