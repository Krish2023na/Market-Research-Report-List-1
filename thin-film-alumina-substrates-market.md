<p><p>The Thin Film Alumina Substrates market includes two main types: Multi Layer and Single Layer. Multi Layer substrates consist of multiple layers of alumina, allowing for better electrical insulation and enhanced performance. These substrates are often used in high-frequency and high-power applications. On the other hand, Single Layer substrates have only one layer, making them less complex but still suitable for many electronic applications. Both Multi Layer and Single Layer substrates play a crucial role in various industries, such as telecommunications, aerospace, and medical devices.</p></p>
