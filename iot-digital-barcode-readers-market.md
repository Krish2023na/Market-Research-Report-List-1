<p><p>In the IoT digital barcode readers market, there are two main types of devices: handheld barcode readers and stationary barcode readers. On the other hand, stationary barcode readers are fixed devices that are installed at specific locations like checkout counters or production lines. Both types of devices play important roles in various industries for inventory management, supply chain, and asset tracking purposes.</p></p>
