<p><p>The Fiberglass Piping System market is divided into several types, including GRE (Glass-Reinforced Epoxy) pipes, GRP (Glass-Reinforced Plastic) pipes, and others. GRE pipes are made by combining epoxy resin with glass fiber reinforcement, offering high resistance to corrosion and excellent mechanical properties. GRP pipes, on the other hand, are constructed utilizing a combination of polyester or vinylester resin with glass fiber reinforcement, providing good chemical resistance and durability. The category "Others" includes alternative types of fiberglass piping systems that may be less commonly used or specialized for specific applications in the market.</p></p>
