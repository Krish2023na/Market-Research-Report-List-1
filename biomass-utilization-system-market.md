<p><p>The Biomass Utilization System market includes various types such as Direct Combustion, Anaerobic Digestion, Fermentation, Oil Extraction, Pyrolysis, and Gasification. Direct combustion refers to burning biomass to produce heat or generate electricity. Anaerobic digestion involves converting organic waste into biogas through the action of bacteria. Fermentation is a process that converts biomass into biofuels or biochemicals. Oil extraction refers to extracting oil from biomass, such as algae. Pyrolysis is the thermal decomposition of biomass to produce bio-oil, gas, and biochar. Gasification converts biomass into a gas called syngas, which can be used for heat, power, or fuel production.</p></p>
