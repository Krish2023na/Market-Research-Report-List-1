<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive optical lens market consists of two main types: spherical lenses and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses have a curved design and are used to correct vision problems such as myopia or hyperopia. They are commonly used in automotive headlights to focus light beams. On the other hand, aspherical lenses have a more complex shape, allowing them to focus light more effectively and reduce optical aberrations. These lenses are used in automotive cameras and sensors to enhance image quality and provide better visibility for advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
