UV-A LEDs emit long-wave ultraviolet light, commonly used in industrial curing, counterfeit detection, and insect traps. UV-B LEDs emit mid-wave ultraviolet light, finding applications in medical phototherapy, sensing, and germicidal applications. UV-C LEDs emit short-wave ultraviolet light and are used for water and air purification, sterilization, and disinfection. Apart from these three types, the UV-LED market also includes applications in fields like agriculture, forensics, and analytical instrumentation.</p></p>
