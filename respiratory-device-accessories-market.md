<p><p>Respiratory device accessories are supplementary components or parts of respiratory devices that are used to enhance the overall functionality, ease of use, and performance of these devices. Manufacturers are focusing on developing lightweight and compact accessories that can be easily carried by patients, enabling them to continue their respiratory therapy while on the go.</p><p>Another trend observed in the market is the integration of advanced technologies in respiratory device accessories.
