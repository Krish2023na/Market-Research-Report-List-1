<p><p>The Dental 3D Printing Scanner market comprises three main types: Intraoral Scanner (IOS), Cone Beam Computerized Tomography (CBCT), and Lab Scanners. Intraoral scanners are handheld devices used by dentists to obtain accurate impressions of patients' teeth and gums inside the mouth. CBCT scanners provide detailed three-dimensional images of teeth, bones, nerves, and soft tissues, enabling diagnosis and treatment planning. Lab Scanners are used by dental laboratories to create digital models of patients' teeth from physical dental impressions, facilitating the design and production of dental prosthetics and restorations.</p></p>
