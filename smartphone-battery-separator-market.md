<p><p>The smartphone battery separator market is categorized into three types - monolayer, bilayer, and others. Monolayer separators are single-layered films that offer excellent thermal stability and higher electrolyte wettability. Bilayer separators consist of two layers, providing enhanced safety features and improved electrolyte retention. On the other hand, the "Others" category encompasses various types of separators like trilayer, ceramic-coated, and nanoporous separators. These separators offer unique properties such as increased puncture resistance, improved thermal stability, and better cycling performance to cater to diverse smartphone battery requirements.</p></p>
