<p><p>The Automotive AGV market comprises three main types: Unit Load Type, Automated Forklift Type, and Tugger Type. The Unit Load Type AGVs are designed to transport heavy loads independently and efficiently within a manufacturing or distribution facility. Automated Forklift Type AGVs are equipped with adaptive forks that allow them to perform the tasks of a conventional forklift with improved efficiency and safety. Tugger Type AGVs are used to tow multiple carts or dollies, making them ideal for material movement in industries such as automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
