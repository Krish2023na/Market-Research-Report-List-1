<p><p>Semiconductor rotary joints are devices used in electronic and radio frequency systems to transmit signals or power between rotating parts. There are two types of semiconductor rotary joints in the market - single channel and multi channel. </p><p>Single channel rotary joints have only one passage to transfer a single signal or power stream. They are suitable for applications requiring low complexity and where only one transmission line is needed.</p><p>On the other hand, multi channel rotary joints have multiple passages to transfer multiple signals or power streams simultaneously. They are ideal for applications where multiple transmission lines are required, allowing for more complex and diverse signal or power transfers.</p></p>
