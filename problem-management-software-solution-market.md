<p><p>Problem management software solutions can be classified into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. </p><p>On-premise solutions are hosted and managed by the organization itself. These solutions are installed on the organization's own servers and infrastructure, allowing them to have complete control and customization over the software. On-premise solutions may require high upfront costs and ongoing maintenance.</p><p>Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, are hosted and managed by third-party providers. These solutions are accessed through the internet and do not require any on-premise hardware or infrastructure. Cloud solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as organizations pay for only the resources they use.</p><p>Both on-premise and cloud-based market types cater to different business needs and preferences, offering various advantages and drawbacks.</p></p>
