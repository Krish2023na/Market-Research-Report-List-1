<p><p>The commercial aircraft maintenance tooling market includes various types of tools used for maintenance and repair of aircraft. Speed handles are used for efficient tightening and loosening of fasteners. Wrenches are essential for tightening bolts and nuts. Safety wire pliers help in securing wires and preventing loosening. Vibration meters measure vibrations to detect faults in aircraft components. Metalworking tools are used for cutting, shaping, and modifying aircraft parts. NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) tools are utilized for inspecting the integrity of aircraft structures. Lastly, "others" include a wide range of tools like torque wrenches, drills, and rivet guns used in different maintenance tasks.</p></p>
