<p><p>The Balloon Biliary Catheters market consists of various types of catheters used in biliary procedures. The Normal Balloon Catheter is a basic type used for dilating narrowed or blocked biliary ducts. Cutting Balloon Catheter has small blades on its surface to safely cut restrictive tissues. Scoring Balloon Catheter has scores or notches to break fibrous strictures. Drug Eluting Balloon Catheter is coated with medication to prevent restenosis. Stent Graft Balloon Catheter is designed for placement of stent grafts in the biliary system to treat strictures and blockages.</p></p>
