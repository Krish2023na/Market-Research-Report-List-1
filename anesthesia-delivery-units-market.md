<p><p>The anesthesia delivery units market can be categorized into two main types: portable and stand-alone units. These units are typically battery-operated and are equipped with features like gas monitoring and vaporizers. On the other hand, stand-alone units are larger in size and are stationary equipment commonly found in hospitals and surgical centers. They are connected to a centralized gas supply system and offer more advanced functions such as integrated monitoring and ventilation capabilities.</p></p>
