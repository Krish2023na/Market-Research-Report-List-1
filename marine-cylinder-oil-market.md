<p><ul><li>High-BN (70-100 BN)</li><li>Low-BN (15-60 BN)</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Marine cylinder oil is classified into two types based on their base number (BN) measurement. High-BN marine cylinder oil has a BN ranging from 70 to 100. This type of oil is commonly used in engines operating under high-stress conditions. On the other hand, low-BN marine cylinder oil has a BN ranging from 15 to 60. This type of oil is typically used in engines operating under normal conditions or where the fuel sulfur content is relatively low. The BN measurement helps determine the oil's ability to neutralize acids formed during combustion.</p></p>
