<p><p>Automotive clutch facings are specialized materials used in the construction of clutches, which help transmit power from the engine to the transmission system. There are two main types of clutch facings: dry type and wet type. Dry type clutch facings are made of materials that do not require lubrication and are suitable for high-speed applications, providing better heat dissipation. On the other hand, wet type clutch facings are designed to work with a lubricating fluid, reducing wear and offering smooth engagement and disengagement.</p></p>
