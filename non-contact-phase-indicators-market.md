<p><p>The non-contact phase indicators market includes two types of devices: the non-contact phase rotation meter and the non-contact phase sequence meter. A non-contact phase rotation meter is used to determine the rotation direction of three-phase electrical networks without the need for physical contact. On the other hand, a non-contact phase sequence meter is designed to identify the correct sequence of phase rotation in three-phase electrical systems without any physical connection. Both devices provide efficient and convenient solutions for electricians and technicians in verifying proper phasing in electrical installations.</p></p>
