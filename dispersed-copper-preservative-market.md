<p><p>The dispersed copper preservative market can be categorized into three types: CBA-A, CA-B, and CA-C. CBA-A refers to copper boron azole type A, which is known for its effectiveness against decay and termite attack in treated wood products. CA-B refers to copper-azole type B, which offers improved protection against termites and decay compared to CBA-A. Lastly, CA-C refers to copper azole type C, which provides enhanced protection against both decay and termites, making it the most effective option among the three.</p></p>
