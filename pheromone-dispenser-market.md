<p><p>Pheromone dispenser market offers various types of traps to efficiently attract and capture insect pests. Bottle traps are containers with a narrow opening that allow pests to enter and get trapped. Funnel traps utilize a funnel-shaped entrance to guide pests into a confined area. Delta traps consist of triangular panels coated with pheromones, attracting pests to stick on the surface. Other trap types include sticky traps, bucket traps, and box traps. Each trap has its unique design and application, but they all aim to effectively lure and control insect populations using pheromones.</p></p>
