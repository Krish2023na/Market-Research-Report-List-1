<p><p>PFPE oils are a type of lubricant used in various industries for their exceptional properties. Four different market types exist within the PFPE oils category: PFPE-K, PFPE-Y, PFPE-D, PFPE-M, and PFPE-Z. PFPE-K oils are known for their outstanding chemical stability and anti-corrosion characteristics. PFPE-Y oils possess excellent thermal stability and perform well under high temperatures. PFPE-D oils are distinguished by their extreme pressure and anti-wear properties. PFPE-M oils stand out for their low volatility and compatibility with oxygen systems. Lastly, PFPE-Z oils offer superior lubrication in challenging operating conditions.</p></p>
