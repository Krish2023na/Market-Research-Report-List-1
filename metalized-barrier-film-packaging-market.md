These films are coated with a thin layer of aluminum to provide barrier properties, like oxygen and moisture resistance, for packaging. Aluminizing polyester film is widely used due to its high strength and flexibility, while aluminizing nylon film offers superior puncture resistance. Aluminizing BOPP provides good heat resistance, aluminizing PE film offers excellent sealing properties, and aluminized paper is commonly used for food packaging.</p></p>
