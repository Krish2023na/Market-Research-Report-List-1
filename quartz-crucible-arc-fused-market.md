<p><p>Quartz crucibles, used in various industries for their exceptional thermal stability, can be classified into two market types: opaque and transparent. Opaque quartz crucibles are made from impure quartz, containing contaminants that give them a milky appearance. These crucibles are ideal for applications where light penetration is not necessary. On the other hand, transparent quartz crucibles are made from pure quartz, giving them excellent clarity. This makes them suitable for applications where visibility is crucial, enabling direct observation of the melting or solidifying processes.</p></p>
