<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical Packaging Aluminum Foil is categorized into two types: Single Zero Aluminum Foil and Double Zero Aluminum Foil. Single Zero Aluminum Foil has a thickness of ≥0.01mm and is commonly used for pharmaceutical blister packaging. It provides excellent barrier properties, ensuring protection against moisture, light, and gases. On the other hand, Double Zero Aluminum Foil has a thickness of ≤0.0075mm and is preferred for its superior malleability and flexibility. It is mostly utilized for deep-drawing applications, such as ampoule seals and syringe body. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining product integrity and extending shelf life.</p></p>
