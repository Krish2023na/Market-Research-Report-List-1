<p><p>The industrial ceramic materials market consists of two main types: ordinary ceramics and special ceramics. Ordinary ceramics are commonly used in everyday applications such as household pottery, kitchenware, and tiles. They are made from natural clay materials and fired at high temperatures to achieve desired properties. On the other hand, special ceramics are engineered materials with superior characteristics such as high strength, resistance to heat and chemicals, and exceptional electrical conductivity. These ceramics are tailored for specific industrial applications like aerospace, automotive, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
