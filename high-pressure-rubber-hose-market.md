<p><p>The high-pressure rubber hose market consists of two main types: spiral wire pressure hose and wire braided pressure hose. Spiral wire pressure hose is reinforced with multiple layers of wire to withstand high pressure and offers excellent flexibility. On the other hand, wire braided pressure hose is reinforced with a single layer of wire, providing moderate flexibility and strength. Both types are widely used in various applications where high pressure and durability are required, such as in hydraulic systems and oil industries.</p></p>
