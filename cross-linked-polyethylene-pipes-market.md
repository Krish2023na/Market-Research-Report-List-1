<p><p>The cross-linked polyethylene pipes market consists of several types of pipes, including Peroxide Cross-Linked Pipe, Silane Cross-Linked Pipe, Irradiation Cross-Linked Pipe, and Azo Cross-Linked Pipe. Peroxide cross-linked pipes are made by adding peroxide to polyethylene, resulting in improved chemical resistance. Silane cross-linked pipes involve the addition of silane compounds, enhancing the thermal and mechanical attributes of the pipes. Irradiation cross-linked pipes undergo high-energy radiation treatment to improve their performance characteristics. Azo cross-linked pipes are created by adding azo compounds, providing excellent dimensional stability and resistance to chemicals, heat, and light.</p></p>
