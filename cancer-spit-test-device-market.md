<p><p>The Cancer Spit Test Device market includes two types: Passive Drool Collection Methods and Oral Swab Collection Methods. Passive Drool Collection Methods involve the use of a device that allows saliva to naturally accumulate in the mouth, which is then directly collected for testing purposes. On the other hand, Oral Swab Collection Methods require the use of swabs to collect saliva from specific areas of the mouth. Both methods aim to provide convenient and non-invasive means of collecting saliva samples for cancer detection and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
