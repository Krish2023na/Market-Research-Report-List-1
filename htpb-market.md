<p><p>HTPB, or Hydroxyl-terminated polybutadiene, is a type of low molecular weight polymer made up of butadiene monomers with hydroxyl functional groups at each end. As a result, it finds diverse applications across various industries such as aerospace, automotive, construction, electronics, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The HTPB market is projected to witness steady growth in the coming years.
